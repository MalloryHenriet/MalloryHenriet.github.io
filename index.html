The Game of Stones

Here are the rules...

You are going to play against a computer

  • There will be a board with a certain NUMBER OF STONES (you can choose this number)
  • You will be able to CHOOSE if you want to start or to let the computer start
  • At each turn you will be able to REMOVE 1, 2 or 3 stones from the board
  • The player who removes the LAST stone wins the game

Good luck !
